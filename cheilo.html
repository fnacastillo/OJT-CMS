Who started Crochei?

Ms. Cheilo Isidro, the visionary behind Crochei, embarked on her entrepreneurial journey by creating intricate crochet pieces, fueled by her passion for craftsmanship and creativity. Initially, she catered to her fellow students, crafting bespoke items that quickly gained popularity within her community. Through dedication and perseverance, Ms. Isidro transformed her hobby into a thriving business, laying the foundation for Crochei's success. Her innovative designs and commitment to quality continue to inspire both novice and experienced crocheters alike, making Crochei a cherished brand in the world of handmade crafts.

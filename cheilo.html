Some people dream of success while other people get up every morning and make it happen.

Wayne Huizenga

Ms. Cheilo Marie Isidro

The owner, a 22-year-old student from Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila, discovered the art of crochet during a particularly stressful semester. What began as a simple hobby soon blossomed into a passion project, providing a much-needed creative outlet amidst the rigors of academia. As she delved deeper into the world of crochet, experimenting with different patterns and yarns, she found solace and joy in the rhythmic motion of her hook. Each stitch became a form of meditation, a respite from the chaos of daily life.

But it wasn't just about relaxation; crochet became a means of self-expression and empowerment. With every completed project, she gained confidence in her skills and discovered a newfound sense of purpose. What started as a way to unwind quickly transformed into a life-changing opportunity. Now, armed with determination and a knack for design, she's turning her passion into a thriving business. Each handmade creation is infused with love and dedication, a testament to her journey from student to entrepreneur. Through her crochet creations, she hopes to inspire others to pursue their passions and embrace the beauty of handmade craftsmanship.
